Hot wheels World Race

Hot Wheels World Race is the first in its series. The movie has you following Vert Wheeler a teen who recently got his license and comes home to a car parked in the driveway with a gps giving him coordinates to a specific location He and other drivers race through different realms to get the "Wheel of power".

Animation

The movie is from 2003 and is completely CGI. This gives the scenes a unique feel to them. The animation on the cars specifically is very well done. The CGI in my opinion has held up pretty well over the years but there are definitely some flaws when it come to misplaced models. Overall I feel like the animation is what makes World Race its identity and is what sets it apart from the series.
